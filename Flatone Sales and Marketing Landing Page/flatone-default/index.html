Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

How to upload your movie to YouTube?

To do it you need to follow the steps:

  1. Create a movie
  2. Open Photos app on your iPad and find the video you created in previous step
  3. Select it and tap on the action button. Tap on the Send to You Tube button.
* If you don’t have a You Tube account you will need to create one.

How do you upload movies to your computer?

1. Create a movie.
2. Sync iPad with your computer and open iTunes
3. Click on the name of your iPad in the left column,
4. Click on the Apps tab on the top
5. Scroll to the bottom until you see File Sharing section
6. Click on the Lecture Capture Icon, you should be able to see the files that are currently used in the app.
7. Your movies have .mov extension. Ctrl-click on one of the .mov files and save it to the disk.
